Resources

Here follows an index of useful resources that might help you on your way to mastering Swedish.

Digital lexicons & dictionaries

Svenska.se

Three official monolingual Swedish dictionaries in one website: use SAOL for spelling and inflections, and SO for definitions, pronunciations and etymology. The third column, SAOB, is for history.

NE.se

The most comprehensive Swedish↔English dictionary, available for a monthly subscription fee or through a library. The dictionary is complete with examples. Also available in ↔German, ↔French, ↔Arabic, ↔Spanish, ↔Russian, ↔Italian, depending on your subscription.

Tyda.se

Most used free Swedish↔English dictionary online. Also available in ↔French, ↔German, ↔Spanish, ↔Latin, ↔Norwegian, ↔Danish.

Wikdict

Multilingual dictionary based on Wiktionary.

Forvo.com

Pronunciation dictionary where the words are pronounced by Swedish natives

The People’s Dictionary

“This English-Swedish dictionary belongs to the people and is expanded and improved by us all.”

Lexin

Translate from Swedish to many other languages. Lexin is made for learners and especially immigrants, with many culturally relevant words.

Synonymer.se

Swedish synonyms

Vocabulary and lists

Kelly

The list of Keywords for Language Learners comprises the 8000+ most common words in Swedish, helping learners focus their efforts. Download the .xls at the bottom of the page.

Parole

‍ An even larger word frequency list, but a bit older

Corpus tools

Korp

A free Swedish language corpus. Useful for looking up common usage of Swedish words and phrases.

KB tidningsarkiv

Look up occurrences of words and phrases in Swedish newspapers over centuries.

Mediearkivet

The most comprehensive corpus of Swedish newspaper and magazine texs, as well as TV and radio news. The search functionality is less sophisticated than Korp, however. Requires login via affiliated library.

TreQ

Parallel corpus, showing how words are commonly translated between languages, including Swedish. Interface in Czech.

Physical books

Swedish: A Comprehensive Grammar

Swedish: A Comprehensive Grammar is an award-winning complete reference guide to modern Swedish grammar. Systematic and accessible, the volume is organised to promote a thorough understanding of Swedish grammar, presenting the complexities of Swedish in a concise and readable form

Form i Fokus

Popular grammar book in Swedish for beginners. Comes in three stages: A, B & C. “Form in Focus combines exercises with a thorough presentation of rules and patterns. Each step is illustrated with clear examples.”

Rivstart

“Rivstart (’Flying start’) is a monolingual beginner- and continuing material in Swedish as a foreign language and is intended for language learners in and outside Sweden. With both humor and seriousness all skills are trained without excessive repetition and unnecessary plodding to fit the study experienced student.”

Målgrammatiken

Swedish range of grammar textbooks, translated and adapted for various source languages, so you can learn Swedish by comparing it to your native language. Available in Polish, Turkish, English, French, Spanish, German, Arabic, Finnish, Persian, Romanian, Russian, Albanian, Thai, Swedish, Somali and Tigrinya. Search for “Svensk grammatik på ” or the author “Åke Viberg”.
